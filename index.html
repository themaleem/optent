About us

We are an event Optimization firm aimed at planning and executing an event taking responsibility for the creative, technical and logistical elements to optimize the experience of an Event.


Our core responsibility is to green your event taking consideration environmental, economic and social issues and still optimizing the memorable experience expected.


We use our event management services as tool for effective strategic marketing and communication so Organizations can benefit from promotional events as a way to reach with current and potential customers.

Event Management

This aspect focuses on using project management technology to establish and grow large scale activities. It includes making research on an event idea, defining the target audience, devising the idea of the event and planning the technical aspects before the event actually begins. Events such as Concerts, formal parties, Product launch, Grand Opening, dinner and anniversary requires planning and co-ordination which varies based on the type of event to be executed. Basic planning includes budgeting, scheduling, site selection, acquiring permits, coordinating transportations and parking, stage setup, decoration, security and catering.


Optimize Entertainment incorporates socially and environmentally responsible decision making into the planning, organization, implementation of, and participation in, an event to ensure all key role players are involved and the event is hosted responsibly.

FEED A MOUTH

if you can't feed a hundred people, then feed just one

Adedoyin Faleye, CEO

Previous Next

This project is an outreach experience to give back to the community, aimed at fighting hunger on the streets and in households which is a major dilemma people with less opportunities face to survive on daily basis.

The first edition of the project was launched on the 30 th of May, 2019 at Egbeda-Iyana ipaja axis and more 300 mouths were reported to have been fed. The second edition happened on the 8th of December, 2019 at Agege, Lagos state and more than 250 people were reported to have received raw food items.



This project was further extended to kebbi state in collaboration with Meenat Foundation to distribute food items and clothing the community in kebbi state cold weather on December 14, 2019. Another project was also completed at Sabo, ile-ife, Osun state with joint effort of Dj Shawry who is a popular female disc jockey from the community.

This project has a ground already and expected to happen all around the country. Donations, support and volunteering open to the public for participation.
